Musical Chairs Rules
  1. To play Musical Chairs, you just need children, chairs and music!
  2. The chairs are placed in a circle, usually facing outwards.&
  3. The adult plays some music which the children dance freely to, and one chair is removed from the circle
  4. When the music stops, the children have to sit on a chair as quickly as possible.
  5. Whoever doesn’t find a chair is out and must wait on the side.
  6. Continue the game until the last child sits on the last chair.
  7. Then, start a new round.
Time Duration Of Game :

The children form a circle around the chairs. Begin playing music. Stop playing the music after a short period of time (approximately 10 to 60 seconds). Everyone in the group should try to find a place for everyone to sit.when the last chair left the game is over
